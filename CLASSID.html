Movie Review

Inception

Inception, directed by Christopher Nolan, is a thrilling science fiction film that delves into the concept of dream manipulation. Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, is a skilled thief who enters the subconscious to steal secrets. With stunning visuals and an engaging story, Inception is a film that leaves you questioning the boundaries between reality and dreams.

Rating: ★★★★★
